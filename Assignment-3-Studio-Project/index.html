    NRG RECORDS WELCOMES FREAK FANTOMS

    Freak Harmonics is a fresh and electrifying band out of New Orleans, Louisiana. Redefining the soundscape with their innovative blend of funk, Psychedelic Rock, and Dark Occult music. Drawing inspiration from legends like Funkadelic, The Isley Brothers, Grateful Dead, and Pink Floyd, they’re reviving the groove with a bold twist.

    The band’s journey began in New Orleans’ historic Tremé neighborhood, where its members— each with roots in the city’s diverse musical traditions—found inspiration in late-night jam sessions that felt almost like spiritual rites. The city’s vibrant culture and haunting history profoundly shaped their music, leading to a fusion of deep grooves, mind-bending psychedelia, and enigmatic lyrics that pay homage to the occult traditions of their hometown. Known for their hypnotic live performances and genre-bending tracks, Freak Harmonics is already generating a buzz in the alternative music scene. "Their music is a transformative experience —a mix of raw energy and cosmic vibes," said a representative from their label. With their debut project on the horizon, Freak Harmonics promises to captivate audiences and carve a unique space in the modern music world.
